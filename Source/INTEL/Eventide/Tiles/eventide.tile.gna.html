What Is GNA?

Gaussian Mixture Model (GMM) co-processors have been integral to Intel silicon since Ice Lake (GNA 1.0) through Arrow Lake (GNA 3.5). As the NPU gained prominence, GNA appeared to be deprecated—until Eventide's audio-first architecture revived the concept.

Where GNA 3.5 focused purely on audio inference with INT8 precision, GNA 5.0 expands the charter:

  • BF16 & INT8 support for wider model compatibility
  • Vision inference for audio-visual context detection
  • Klangkerne integration for seamless audio processing
  • 14A-E process node for minimum leakage power and always-on viability

Always-On Audio AI in Action
